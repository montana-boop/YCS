# Single Besties — Store Copy

Ready-to-paste copy for the Single Besties store. All text avoids em dashes and
states shipping as 7 to 14 business days. Customer service contact is
jesse@straughterco.com.

---

## 1. Shipping Policy
Paste into Settings > Policies > Shipping policy (or a dedicated page).

**Shipping Policy**

Every Single Besties order is made and shipped with care. Please allow 7 to 14
business days for your order to be processed and shipped.

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking so you
can follow it all the way to your door.

**Shipping times**
- Processing and shipping: 7 to 14 business days
- You will get a tracking link by email as soon as your order is on its way

**Where we ship**
We currently ship within the United States. If you have a question about your
location, just reach out before you order.

**Wrong address or need a change?**
Contact us as soon as possible at jesse@straughterco.com with your order number.
We will do our best to update it before your order ships. We are not able to
change an address once a package is on its way.

**Lost, delayed, or damaged packages**
If your tracking has not updated or your order arrives damaged, email
jesse@straughterco.com and we will help you sort it out.

**Questions about your order?**
Reach our customer service team any time at jesse@straughterco.com and we will be
happy to help.

## 2. Abandoned-cart email
Use in Marketing > Automations > Abandoned checkout (or Settings > Checkout >
Abandoned checkout email). Shopify auto-inserts the item list and a Return to cart
button.

**Subject line options**
- You left something behind, bestie
- Your cart misses you
- Still thinking about it? Your picks are waiting

**Preview text**
Your Single Besties picks are still in your cart. Grab them before they are gone.

**Body**

Hi bestie,

Looks like you left a few things in your cart. No pressure, but your picks are
still here waiting for you.

Everything ships within 7 to 14 business days, and you will get tracking by email
as soon as your order is on its way.

Ready when you are. Just tap the button below to pick up right where you left off.

[Return to cart]

Questions about your order or sizing? Email us any time at
jesse@straughterco.com and we are happy to help.

With love,
Single Besties

**Optional second reminder (send about 24 hours later)**

Subject: Last call on your cart, bestie

Hi bestie,

Your cart is still saved, but we cannot hold it forever. If you have been thinking
it over, now is a great time to make it yours.

Orders ship in 7 to 14 business days. Need help before you check out? Reach us at
jesse@straughterco.com.

[Return to cart]

With love,
Single Besties

## 3. Order Confirmation
Paste into Settings > Notifications > Order confirmation. Add this as the intro
message above the order summary. Shopify fills in the order number, items, and
totals automatically.

**Subject**
Order confirmed, bestie. We are on it.

**Body**

Hi {{ customer.first_name }},

Thank you for your order. We are so glad you are here.

Your order is confirmed and our team is getting it ready. Please allow 7 to 14
business days for your order to be processed and shipped. As soon as it is on its
way, we will send you a shipping confirmation with tracking.

You will find your full order details below.

Need to make a change or have a question? Email us at jesse@straughterco.com with
your order number and we will take care of you.

With love,
Single Besties

## 4. Shipping Confirmation
Paste into Settings > Notifications > Shipping confirmation. Add this as the intro
message above the tracking details. Shopify fills in the tracking number and link
automatically.

**Subject**
Your Single Besties order is on the way

**Body**

Hi {{ customer.first_name }},

Great news, your order has shipped and is on its way to you.

You can follow your package using the tracking link below. Please note that
tracking can take a day or two to show its first update.

If anything looks off with your delivery, or you have a question about your order,
email us any time at jesse@straughterco.com and we will help you sort it out.

Thank you for shopping with us.

With love,
Single Besties
