## Assumptions

- Print costs are your Tekton figures from July 2, 2026 — confirm the "(6)" note on Rich Auntie Tee isn't a per-6-unit price before treating it as final.
- Shipping is modeled at $2.75 for light items (tees/tank/hat/totes) and $4.25 for hoodies/longsleeve — the midpoint of Shopify's discounted USPS Ground Advantage range, not a confirmed invoice figure.
- Tote print type is assumed: Don't Text Tote = front+back ($15 cost), Solo Date Tote = front only ($12 cost). Swap the rows if that's reversed.
- Excludes payment processing (~2.9% + $0.30/order), packaging materials, and ad spend.
- Catalog note: "Rich Auntie Longsleeve" and "Baby Cakes Hoodie" are both set up in Shopify as product type *Crewneck Sweatshirt* — a naming quirk only, no effect on the math.
